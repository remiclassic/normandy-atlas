## Surveys and starting points

- **Normandy:** David Bates, *Normandy Before 1066*; *William the Conqueror* (Yale English Monarchs). Lucien Musset, *Les peuples scandinaves au VIIIe au XIe siècle* (context for Scandinavian settlement).
- **England:** David C. Douglas, *William the Conqueror*; R. Allen Brown, *The Normans and the Norman Conquest*; Marjorie Chibnall, *Anglo-Norman England*; Hugh M. Thomas, *The English and the Normans*.
- **France / Angevin:** John Gillingham, *The Angevin Empire*; Kate Norgate, older but still cited for political narrative—supplement with current articles on 1204.
- **Italy / Sicily:** Graham Loud, *The Age of Robert Guiscard*; Donald Matthew, *The Norman Kingdom of Sicily*; Jeremy Johns, *Arabic administration in Norman Sicily* (themes).
- **Crusades / Antioch:** Ralph-Johannes Lilie, *Byzantium and the Crusader States*; accessible narratives in *Cambridge History of the Crusades* volumes.
- **Wales / Ireland:** R. R. Davies, *Domination and Conquest*; Robin Frame, *Colonial Ireland, 1169–1369*.

## How to use this list

This is a **starter shelf**, not exhaustive. For any pin on the map, prefer recent monographs and excavation reports for that site; use the thematic essays above for framing.
