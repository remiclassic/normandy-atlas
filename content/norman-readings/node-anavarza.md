## Anavarza

The atlas summarizes this site as: **Ancient site strengthened by Crusaders with heavy stone defenses**. This essay is a **reading companion**: it frames what “Norman” can mean politically and architecturally here, without replacing specialist monographs or excavation reports.

**Identity:** Use lordship, charters, and phases of building—not modern categories of “blood.” The map shows geography and modeled movement; nuance lives in the sources.
