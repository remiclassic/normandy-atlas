## Custom of Normandy

Ducal Normandy developed a distinctive legal culture recorded in later treatises and charters. After 1066, English law and Norman custom interacted under kings who were also dukes—institutional bilingualism in law as in court culture.

## Church courts and laity

Ecclesiastical jurisdiction mattered for marriage, tithe, and moral offenses; secular courts for land and violence. The map cannot show jurisdictions; treat routes and pins as geography only.
